Card Inventory Manager

A fairly simple yet effective inventory manager I made specifically for Pokemon card inventory management for card vendors. It features a rugged barebones UI, but does exactly what it was made for. It allows the user to create entries of trading cards (name, number, condition, buy price) and creates a database entry for them, which is then displayed in an Excel-like fashion. It also automatically generates a 12-digit barcode with accompanying png for labeling purposes. Each entry can also be "Sold" in which after entering a sold amount and submitting, moves that entry into the "Sold Card" database. Although this project has little to do with my area of expertise, It was very enjoyable and offered a good perspective into frontend and user-friendly development.
